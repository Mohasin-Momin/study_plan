Salary negotiation guide
Target ranges (India 2026, your profile)
Pune product/GCC: ₹18–28 LPA (current est. ~12–16 LPA)
Bangalore product startup: ₹22–35 LPA
Remote (US/EU firm): $45K–80K/yr in USD
Negotiation rules
Never give current salary first — deflect to expected CTC
Always negotiate — first offer is rarely the best offer
Get competing offers — even one rival offer shifts leverage
Negotiate joining bonus for a smooth transition
Ask about ESOPs, variable component, learning budget
Week 15–16
Offer Evaluation + Transition Planning
Making the right choice matters more than winning the offer
~68 hrs
Offer evaluation checklist
Base CTC — is it ≥40% hike from current?
Variable component — what % and how achievable?
Role title — Senior DS vs ML Engineer vs AI Engineer matters for next hop
Tech stack — do you want to keep using what you know or learn new?
Team size and manager quality — biggest career impact
Growth trajectory — is there a Principal/Lead path?
Domain — pharma AI vs fintech vs general? Your niche = premium elsewhere
Remote policy — fully remote, hybrid, or Pune only?
Final week tasks
Accept best offer in writing, negotiate start date for buffer
Knowledge transfer at current role — leave on good terms
Keep GitHub and LinkedIn updated with AI-103 cert (if passed)
Enroll in AI-300 prep course for next 3 months post-joining
Set up home dev environment before Day 1 at new company
Connect with future teammates on LinkedIn before Day 1
